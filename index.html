Sun Flower Image

The sunflower (Helianthus annuus) is a cheerful bloom with striking golden petals and a rich brown center. Known for its unique ability to follow the sun—a phenomenon called heliotropism—this vibrant flower symbolizes warmth, loyalty, and positivity. Often seen swaying in sunny fields, sunflowers attract bees and butterflies, adding life to any garden. Beyond their beauty, sunflower seeds are a tasty snack and a key ingredient in cooking oil, making these flowers both delightful and practical!
